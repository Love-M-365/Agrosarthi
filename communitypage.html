Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • You can register on Agrosarthi by clicking the 'Register' button on the homepage and filling out the required details in the registration form.

  • To ask a question to an expert, go to the Expert Q&A section, select a topic, and submit your question through the provided form.

  • Participate in AMA sessions by checking the upcoming events in the AMA section and submitting your question in advance or during the live session.

  • You can privately message another farmer by going to their profile page and clicking on the 'Send Message' button.

  • You can access regional forums by navigating to the 'Forums' section and selecting your region from the list of available forums.
